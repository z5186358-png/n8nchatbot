Our structure

Top

Heart

Base

What lingers

Woods, amber, and musk anchor the blend, staying in the room long after the flame is out.

Where it starts

A workbench in Portland, not a factory line.

Ombre & Ash began as a single melting pot and a notebook of scent formulas, tested on friends before anything was sold. That bench is still where every formula starts today.

We work exclusively with coconut-soy wax, cotton wicks, and fragrance oils free of phthalates — chosen for a clean burn as much as for the room they leave behind.

How a candle is made

From formula to finished pour

01

Formulate

Top, heart, and base notes are weighed and blended by hand, then rested for 48 hours.

02

Pour

Wax is melted, blended with the formula, and poured into vessels at a controlled temperature.

03

Cure

Each candle rests for ten days so the scent binds evenly through the wax before it's tested.

04

Test & pack

We burn one from every batch for four hours, checking throw and flame, before packing by hand.

Before you write in

A few common questions

How long does a candle burn?

Every candle in the collection is rated for 45–55 hours, tested in ten-day cure batches before sale.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship to most countries; wax items are shipped in insulated packaging in warmer months.

Can I return an opened candle?

If a scent isn't right for you, we accept returns within 30 days, opened or not.
